Final Project Proposal

Topic

My website is going to compile my little brother's favorite video games and specific information about the game or characters in the game that my brother loves.

Audience

For those who have a love for the games that my brother plays, specifically the mainstream video games on the Nintendo Switch.

Design

I was going for a poppy design, one that has catches the eye with colorful schemes that matches each page of video game aesthetic.

Color Palette:

Bottle Green
Neon Yellow
Bright Orange
Peach Pink
Hot Deep Pink
Blue Whale

Website Inspiration:

  • https://www.smashbros.com/
  • https://www.WorldofMineCraft.com/
  • https://www.geekysweetie.com/

Scope

I want around 4 pages, or at least 3 sections per three video games.

  1. Minecraft
    • About
    • Worlds
    • Characters
  2. Super Smash Bros.
    • About
    • Favorite Characters
  3. Pokemon Violet
    • About
    • Favorite Pokemon
    • New Updates!

I was going to have a big background for each video game, to show which video game I was going to talk about, for each section or page I will have a big background.

In each of the About pages, I want to add background information about each game, and help the audience understand what each video game is about.

In the Worlds for Minecraft, I wanted to include online creations or my brother's creations in the section.

In the favorite characters or favorite pokemon, I wanted to include images of my brother's favorite characters from each game, and I wanted to write a little bit about each character.
